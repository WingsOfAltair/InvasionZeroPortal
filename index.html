ABOUT THE GAME

Invasion Zero is a multi-player online free-to-play first-person shooter game supporting up to 8 players per session. Play against enemy AI bots or test your skills against players from all over the world.

Equip yourself with a machine gun, shotgun, or sniper rifle depending on your class, along with a knife and hand grenades. Your objective is to survive the battle and destroy spawning enemy forces in intense combat scenarios.

GAME MODES

Team Deathmatch (TDM)

Engage in intense team-based combat. Work with your teammates to eliminate the opposing force and dominate the battlefield.

Free-for-All (FFA)

Every player for themselves. Rely only on your skills as you battle alone against all opponents in a chaotic arena.

Capture The Flag (CTF)

Strategy meets combat. Secure your team's flag while defending your base and attacking the enemy's stronghold.

Gun Race (GR)

Progress through weapon tiers as you eliminate opponents. First to reach the final weapon wins the race.

Domination/Cover Point (CP)

Control strategic positions on the map. Hold multiple points to earn points and secure victory for your team.

Search and Destroy (SND)

Tactical gameplay where teams take turns attacking and defending. One life per round - precision and strategy are key.

Elimination

Test your survival skills in pure combat elimination rounds. Last player standing claims victory.

Demolition

Plant or defuse explosives in high-stakes tactical gameplay. Teamwork and communication are essential.
